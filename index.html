What is a NEO?

A near-Earth object is an asteroid or comet whose orbit brings it close to Earth's neighbourhood around the Sun.

⚠️

"Potentially hazardous"

A formal NASA label for larger objects whose orbits pass especially close to Earth — not a prediction of impact.

🛰️

Planetary defense

Missions like DART prove we can nudge an asteroid's orbit — making tracking the first line of defense.
